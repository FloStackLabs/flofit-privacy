FLOFIT Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

FLOFIT is a workout tracking app built around a simple principle: your data is yours, and it never leaves your device.

The short version

FLOFIT collects nothing, transmits nothing, and has no servers. The app has no internet access at all — not as a promise, but as a technical fact: it does not request the network permission, so the operating system does not allow it to communicate over the internet.

What the app stores — on your device only

FLOFIT stores the information you enter so the app can function:

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, weights, reps, times, distances)
  • Body measurements you record (such as weight or body fat)
  • Routines, exercise library entries, and personal records
  • App settings and preferences (units, theme, goals, equipment)

All of this lives in a local database on your device. It is never uploaded, synced, or shared by the app. We (the developer) cannot see it, access it, or recover it — we never receive it in the first place.

What we collect: nothing

  • No account or sign-up exists.
  • No analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs are included.
  • No advertising of any kind.
  • No third-party services receive any data, because the app cannot reach the network.

Backups and export

FLOFIT includes a manual export feature. When you export your data, a backup file is created and you choose where it goes (for example, a folder on your device or a service you select). That file is under your control; the app does not send it anywhere itself. FLOFIT does not participate in automatic cloud backup — the manual export is the only copy of your data that exists outside the app.

Permissions

The app requests a single permission: vibration, used for haptic feedback when you interact with controls. It requests no others — no internet, no location, no contacts, no camera, no storage beyond what you explicitly choose during export.

Health and fitness data

The workout and body-metric information you enter is health-related data. It is treated like everything else in FLOFIT: stored locally, never transmitted, never shared.

Deleting your data

Your data is deleted when you clear the app's storage or uninstall the app. Because no copy ever reaches us, there is nothing for us to delete on any server — no request or waiting period is needed.

Children

FLOFIT is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone (it does not collect information at all).

Changes to this policy

If a future version of FLOFIT ever changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated and the effective date revised before that version is released.
